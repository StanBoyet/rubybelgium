Rails Girls Brussels

The basics of programming, for girls, in a day!
Rails Girls was born in Finland, but is now a global, non-profit volunteer community. Our goal is to give girls and women enough tools to understand technology and a supportive community to help them build their ideas. We do this by organizing a 1 evening + 1 day ruby bootcamp with online training materials and coaches to help them along the way.

Getting started with tech, like a breeze

You're in good hands, all our coaches from the community are there to help you!

You will learn designing, prototyping and coding with help from our coaches.

You only need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!
